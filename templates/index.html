You can watch the top companies of New York Stock Exchange and buy their stock at current price.

1yr Target Est - This is the estimate for the stock price after 1 year of stock trading done by Yahoo finance. It takes in account the news, stock behaviour, dividend, etc. For beginners it's a good place to start !

Current Price - This is your actual current stock price. You would be making the trade on these stocks.

Total Revenue - Revenue is simply the amount of money flowing into a company as a result of the sale of goods and services. Revenue is the top line of an income statement. It is the total sum of positive cash flow.

Earning per Share - A company's profit divided by its number of common outstanding shares.

Direction of Stock - Beginner only.

Their are plenty of other factors that have to be taken in account before making responsible stock trading decisions. Please refer to Google links and Yahoo links.
